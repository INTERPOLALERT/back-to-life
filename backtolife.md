2\. WHY THIS APP WILL WORK FOR YOU {#why-this-works}

The Research Shows These Specific Approaches Work:

1\. ONE THING AT A TIME (Research: Cognitive Load Theory)



Your brain can't handle multiple tasks or long lists

ONE task per day prevents overwhelm

Small wins rebuild self-trust



2\. SECOND-BY-SECOND GUIDANCE (Research: Executive Function Support)



You don't need to remember what's next

The app tells you EXACTLY what to do right now

No decisions = no paralysis



3\. GAMIFICATION (Research: Behavioral Activation + Reward Systems)



Your champion brain responds to quests and levels

Tiny victories feel like progress

XP and achievements replace shame with pride



4\. START MICROSCOPIC (Research: Graded Task Assignment)



"Touch the door" not "go for a walk"

"Open PDF, read one sentence" not "study for exams"

Impossible to fail = builds momentum



5\. BODY BEFORE MIND (Research: Physical Rehabilitation)



Your brain fog is partly from 6 years sitting

Oxygen to brain improves everything

Movement = better mood, focus, memory



6\. NO CHOICE OVERWHELM (Research: Decision Fatigue Prevention)



The app decides for you

You just do what it says

Removes analysis paralysis





3\. CORE DESIGN PRINCIPLES {#core-principles}

How BackToLife Is Built For YOUR Brain:

Principle 1: THE APP KNOWS YOU MORE THAN YOU KNOW YOURSELF



Pre-loaded with EVERYTHING from our conversation

Understands your patterns, fears, and triggers

Adapts based on how you're doing each day

Never asks "What do you want to do?" - it TELLS you



Principle 2: RADICAL SIMPLICITY



Simple words (7th grade reading level max)

No medical jargon

Clear buttons and screens

Can't get lost in menus



Principle 3: ONE PRIMARY QUEST PER DAY



Wake up ‚Üí see ONE thing to do

Complete it ‚Üí done for the day (bonus quests optional)

No guilt if you only do the one thing

Each quest takes 30 seconds to 5 minutes MAX



Principle 4: IMPOSSIBLE TO FAIL



Quests so small they seem silly

"Stand up for 10 seconds" = valid quest

If you fail, quest gets even easier

Celebrates EVERY completion



Principle 5: CHAMPION LANGUAGE



Speaks to you like you're training for a comeback

Not therapy talk, but coach talk

"Your champion self is waiting" not "you should try harder"



Principle 6: DARK MODE, ADHD-FRIENDLY



No overwhelming colors or animations

Big text, clear icons

Focus mode (blocks distractions)

Audio options (text-to-speech for when reading is hard)



Principle 7: PRIVACY \& SAFETY



All data stored locally on your phone

No cloud sync required (optional)

No one sees your struggles

Emergency "panic button" for crisis moments
